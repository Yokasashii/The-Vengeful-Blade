## The story of The Vengeful Blade:

In a kingdom torn apart by injustice, a young prisoner nicknamed “The Little Knight” manages to escape from his cell.
Pursued by the king's guards, he rushes into a luxurious hall where three legendary weapons await him.
The choice he makes will determine his class... and his destiny.

Driven by a desire for revenge against the king, whom he accuses of destroying his family, he sets off on an adventure.
Battle after battle, zone after zone, he gains strength and experience, gradually getting closer to his goal: overthrowing the sovereign.

But at the end of his journey, facing the final boss, the truth will be revealed.
The king was not the enemy he thought he was, but a guardian protecting the kingdom from an evil entity.
His death paves the way for this ancient evil, ready to plunge the world into chaos.
